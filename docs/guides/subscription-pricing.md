## Which Plan Is Right for You?

**Free** — Explore the dashboard and documentation. No trading capabilities.

**Starter** — Best for individual traders automating 1-3 strategies on up to 2 broker accounts. Good for testing the platform with real money after your trial.

**Trader** — For active traders running multiple strategies across several accounts. Includes marketplace selling and expanded copy trading groups. The most popular plan.

**Unlimited** — For professional traders and firms with no limits on accounts, webhooks, or strategies. Full copy trading and marketplace access.

## 7-Day Free Trial

All paid plans (Starter, Trader, Unlimited) include a 7-day free trial:

- Full access to all features of the selected plan
- No charge until the trial period ends
- Cancel anytime during the trial — you won't be billed
- After the trial, you're automatically billed at the selected plan's rate

## How to Subscribe

1. Go to [atomiktrading.io](https://atomiktrading.io) and click **Get Started**
2. Choose your plan and billing cycle (monthly or yearly)
3. Complete checkout via Stripe (credit/debit card)
4. Your account is immediately activated with full access

## Managing Your Subscription

### Upgrade or Downgrade

You can change your plan at any time:

1. Go to **Settings > Billing** in your dashboard
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. Select your new plan

Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

### Cancel

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Manage Subscription**
2. Click **Cancel Plan**
3. You retain access until the end of your current billing period

### Billing Portal

Click **Manage Subscription** to access the Stripe billing portal where you can:

- Update payment method
- View invoice history
- Download receipts
- Change billing cycle

## Payment Failure Handling

If a payment fails (expired card, insufficient funds, etc.):

1. **Grace period** — You retain full access while we retry the payment
2. **Warning** — Email notification about the failed payment
3. **Urgent** — Follow-up notification if still unresolved
4. **Final notice** — Last chance to update your payment method
5. **Suspended** — Account access is restricted until payment is resolved

**To resolve:** Update your payment method in **Settings > Billing > Manage Subscription**. Once payment succeeds, full access is immediately restored.

## FAQ

**Can I switch plans mid-cycle?**
Yes. Upgrades are prorated and take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the next billing date.

**What happens to my strategies if I downgrade?**
If your new plan has lower limits, excess strategies/webhooks will be deactivated. You choose which ones to keep.

**Is there a refund policy?**
Contact [support@atomiktrading.io](mailto:support@atomiktrading.io) within 30 days for refund requests.

**Do yearly plans auto-renew?**
Yes. You can cancel anytime before renewal through the billing portal.
